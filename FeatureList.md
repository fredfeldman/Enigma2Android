# E2Android — Feature List

Control your Enigma2 satellite or cable receiver (Dreambox, VU+, Octagon, GigaBlue, etc.) from your Android tablet.

---

## Live TV
- Browse all bouquets and channel lists from your receiver
- Tap any channel to stream it instantly in full-screen
- Channel picon (logo) support
- Now/Next programme display with live progress bar per channel
- Quick-filter / search channels by name
- Favourites bouquet — star any channel to save it
- Screenshot your receiver's current picture from the toolbar

## Full-Screen Player
- Smooth HLS / MPEG-TS playback via ExoPlayer
- Tap the screen to show/hide the on-screen display (OSD)
- OSD auto-hides after 5 seconds
- Previous / Next channel buttons — browse without leaving the player
- Audio track selection (AC3, stereo, multi-language)
- Subtitle / Closed Caption track selection
- Aspect ratio cycling: Fit · Fill · Zoom · 16:9 · 4:3
- Sleep timer: 15 / 30 / 60 minutes
- Stop button always visible — one tap to exit

## EPG (Electronic Programme Guide)
- Full 2D grid view across all channels
- Pinned channel column with horizontal time scrolling
- Tap to view programme details
- Double-tap to play the channel immediately
- Long-press to schedule a recording timer

## EPG Search
- Search programme titles across all channels
- Results show channel, date/time and title
- Tap a result to go straight to the programme or set a timer

## Recordings
- Browse all recordings stored on your receiver
- Sort by date (newest/oldest), title or channel
- Detail panel shows description, channel, date and duration
- Full-screen playback with seek bar scrubbing
- Rewind / Fast-forward 15 seconds
- Resume from last watched position

## Timers
- View all scheduled recording timers on the receiver
- See timer status: waiting, recording, completed or failed
- Delete timers directly from the app

## Multiple Receivers
- Store unlimited receiver profiles
- Switch between receivers with a single tap
- Per-device host, port, username, password and HTTPS settings
- Wake-on-LAN — power on your receiver from deep standby

## Playlists
- Create and name local playlists of recordings
- Add recordings to playlists with a long-press
- Drag to reorder playlist entries
- Auto-play next item when one recording finishes
- Browse receiver HDD / NAS folders directly

## Notifications
- Background polling every 15 minutes
- Push notification when a recording timer starts

## Settings & Customisation
- Dark theme (default) and light theme
- Hide bouquets you don't use
- Auto-resume recordings from last position
- Per-device credentials — supports HTTP Basic Auth

## Requirements
- Android 8.0 or newer
- Android tablet with touchscreen
- Enigma2 receiver running OpenWebif
